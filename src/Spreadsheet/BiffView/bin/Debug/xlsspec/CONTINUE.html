CONTINUE: Continues Long Records (3Ch)

Records longer than 8,228 bytes (2,084 bytes in BIFF7 and earlier) must be split into several records. The first section appears in the base record; subsequent sections appear in CONTINUE records.

In BIFF8, the TXO record is always followed by CONTINUE records that store the string data and formatting runs.
