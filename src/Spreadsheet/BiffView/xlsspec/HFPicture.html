HFPicture: Header / Footer Picture (866h)

Introducted in Excel 10 (2002) this BIFF record is an FRT record. It includes an embedded encoding of the contents of the picture, which may be in MSODRAWING or MSODRAWINGGROUP record format.  Pictures contain large amounts of data and may be continued across multiple HFPICTURE records (see fContinue flag).
