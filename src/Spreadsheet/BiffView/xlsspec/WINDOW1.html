WINDOW1: Window Information (3Dh)

The WINDOW1 record contains workbook-level window attributes. The xWn and yWn fields contain the location of the window in units of 1/20 th of a point, relative to the upper-left corner of the Excel window client area. The dxWn and dyWn fields contain the window size, also in units of 1/20 th of a point.
